Mapping Your Market in 6 Easy Steps

1. Define Scope

Specify your target market, product category, and audience.

2. Identify Drivers

Determine core value drivers influencing customer decisions.

3. Weight Drivers

Use pairwise comparisons to find the relative importance of each driver.

4. Add Products

Include details like brand, price, and image for each product.

5. Rate Performance

Score products on each value driver to assess strengths and weaknesses.

6. Visualize Map

Generate a market map to identify price points and market gaps.
