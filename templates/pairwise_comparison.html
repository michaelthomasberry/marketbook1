Understanding Pairwise Comparison Methodology

Think of building a house. You have many features you could include (a big kitchen, a home office, a large garden). Pairwise comparison helps you decide which features are most valuable to the homeowner. Do they value a big kitchen more than a large garden? By comparing these features two at a time, you can determine the true priorities and ensure you build a house that meets their needs.
